New Vocabulary

Pollution (n)

The process of making air, water, soil dirty.

Aquatic (adj)

Growing or living in, on, or near water.

Habitat (n)

The natural home of a plant or animal.

Endangered (adj)

At risk of no longer existing (extinction).
